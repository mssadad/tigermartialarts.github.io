Who We Are

Tiger Martial Arts is a premier fitness and self-defense academy based in Bur Dubai. We provide top-tier training in martial arts, fitness, and self-defense in a supportive, inclusive environment.

Our facility includes a fully-matted martial arts hall, modern gym, sauna, steam room, and dedicated locker rooms. Whether you're a beginner or a seasoned martial artist, we have programs tailored for all levels.
