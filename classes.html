Our Martial Arts Programs

Explore a wide range of classes for all ages and skill levels at Tiger Martial Arts.

Brazilian Jiu-Jitsu

A grappling-based martial art focused on ground fighting, submissions, and control. Perfect for self-defence and competition.

Karate

A traditional Japanese art focusing on striking, discipline, and kata. Great for building confidence and coordination.

Muay Thai

Known as the “Art of Eight Limbs,” Muay Thai trains students in powerful kicks, punches, knees, and elbows.

Judo

An Olympic sport that focuses on throws and takedowns, ideal for both fitness and self-control development.

Kids Martial Arts

Fun, safe classes tailored for children aged 5–14, focused on respect, focus, and self-esteem.

Private Tuition

One-on-one sessions customized to meet your fitness or martial arts goals at your own pace.
